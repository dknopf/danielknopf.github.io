About Me!

I'm a junior at Wesleyan University double majoring in computer science and English. I am a software engineer for the Wesleyan Privacy Tech Lab and the student-created professor review app OurCampus, a course assistant for several high level computer science classes, and a co-founder of Wesleyan's hackathon, WesHack!
